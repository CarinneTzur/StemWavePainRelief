About Stemwave

Relief built around you

Helping your body return to what it does naturally.

Stemwave Pain Relief offers advanced Stemwave Therapy for chronic, nagging pain in areas such as the knees, neck, lower back, hands, elbows, feet, plantar fasciitis, and more.

Led by Stemwave provider Joe Wright, this non-surgical, minimally invasive therapy is designed to support the body’s natural healing response and help clients move forward with less pain and more freedom.
